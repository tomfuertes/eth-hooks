Quick Start Guide - 5min ⏱️

Fast & Easy Web3 React Hooks

Speed up your frontend development with pre-built hooks for use with EthersAppContext, allowing easy use throughout your application. Additionally, works out of the box with scaffold-eth-typescript template!

Optimized Web3 Development

Dedicated caching reduces RPC calls. Use a variety of web3 network update options. Typed contracts increase the clarity of the contract interface. These are just a few of the benefits you get with eth-hooks.

Seemless Smart Contract Interaction

Use the ContractAppContext and ethers.js to have typed access to your smart contracts. This gives you reliable read and write controls to smart contracts and even lets you pass different ethers providers.
